8Pro Plan Expiry & Grace Period

8.1 Downgrade on expiry

When your Pro subscription expires or is cancelled, your account reverts to the Free plan immediately. Pro features (analytics, Gmail Import, CSV export, archive actions) are hidden at once.

8.2 Seven-day grace period for reminders

Your active Expense Reminders remain active for 7 days after expiry. After this grace period, a nightly automated process archives all reminders except the one created first, reducing your account to the Free-plan limit of 1 active reminder.

8.3 Data visibility after expiry

  • Your historical data (payment logs, analytics, archived entries) remains visible in the app for 180 days after expiry.
  • After 180 days the data is hidden from the app but retained in our database for up to 365 days to allow reactivation.
  • After 365 days from expiry, all data is permanently and automatically deleted from our systems.

8.4 Reactivation

You may re-subscribe at any time. If you resubscribe within 365 days of expiry, your historical data will be restored. After 365 days, data cannot be recovered.
