When you cancel:

  • Your Pro access remains active until the end of the current paid period.
  • You will not be charged again unless you choose to renew.
  • After the paid period ends, your account automatically downgrades to the Free plan.

Plan Expiry & Downgrade

If your Pro plan expires (either through cancellation or non-renewal) your account transitions to the Free plan. The following changes apply immediately upon downgrade:

  • All Pro-only features (full analytics, unlimited reminders beyond 1, CSV export, payday alignment) are hidden from the App interface.
  • Your existing reminder data remains stored in the backend for 180 days following the expiry date.
  • If you renew your Pro plan within 180 days of expiry, your previous reminder data becomes accessible again.
  • If you do not renew within 180 days, reminder and notification data is permanently deleted in accordance with Section 8 (Data Retention).
⚠️ Important: After 180 days without renewal, reminder data cannot be recovered. We recommend exporting your data via CSV before allowing your Pro plan to expire.
