1. Information We Collect

We collect the minimum information necessary to provide the Align service.

Information you provide directly:

  • Account information - your name, email address, and profile photo provided through Google Sign-In.
  • Reminder data - bill names, amounts, due dates, payment frequencies, categories, and notes you enter into the App.
  • Payment information - subscription plan selections and transaction references processed through Razorpay. We do not store your payment card or UPI details directly.

Information collected automatically:

  • Device information - device model, operating system version, app version, and technical diagnostic information required for troubleshooting and security.
  • Usage data - feature interactions, crash diagnostics, and performance metrics may be collected in aggregated or pseudonymised form to improve app reliability and user experience. We do not track individual browsing behaviour.
  • Push notification tokens - device tokens required to deliver bill reminders to your device.

Information we do NOT collect:

  • Bank account numbers, credit card details, or UPI credentials.
  • Location data or GPS coordinates.
  • Contacts, photos, or files from your device.
  • Browsing history outside the App.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on Supabase's secure cloud infrastructure. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest using AES-256 encryption.

We implement Row-Level Security (RLS) policies ensuring that each user can only access their own data. API keys and secrets are never stored in the App's source code.

The App also stores a local offline cache on your device using MMKV encrypted storage. This enables reminders and notifications to function without an internet connection.

Additionally, the App may rely on Google Play Services and related Google infrastructure to provide authentication, notifications, security, and device compatibility features.

🔐 While we take reasonable precautions to protect your data, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong Google account password and keep your device updated.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data according to the following schedule to balance user control, privacy, and account history continuity:

Data Type Retention Period Future Sign-In Restore Behavior
Reminder & Notification Data (bill names, amounts, due dates, reminder days, notes, push tokens, notification logs) Retained for up to 180 days following account deletion request or downgrade from Pro to Free plan, then permanently and automatically removed from the database server. Not restored if 180 days have passed. If signed in again within 180 days, this data is restored.
Profile & Payment Data (User profile details, email, payment records, transaction history, subscription history) Permanently retained on the database server for the lifetime of the account history. Fully restored and displayed immediately upon subsequent sign-ins.

After the 180-day period following deletion or downgrade, reminder and notification data is permanently and irreversibly removed from our systems. Payment records, transaction details, and user profiles are retained permanently for legal, accounting, and user recovery purposes.

6. Your Rights & Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access - Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction - Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion - Request deletion of your account via Account → Delete Account in the App. Your reminder and notification data will be retained for 180 days before being permanently deleted, while profile and payment records remain stored permanently to support future sign-ins.
  • Portability - Export your reminder data as a CSV file via the Analytics → Export Report feature (Pro users).
  • Withdraw consent - Disable push notifications at any time via your device settings or Account → Notification Preferences.
  • Object - Object to processing of your data for legitimate interest purposes.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at alignapps4@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Push Notifications

Align uses push notifications to deliver bill reminders directly to your device. Notifications are scheduled locally on your device and can fire without an internet connection.

You can control notifications at any time:

  • Via Account → Notification Preferences in the App.
  • Via your device's Settings → Apps → Align → Notifications.

Disabling notifications does not affect your reminder data stored in the App. Your Notification Inbox continues to show all notifications regardless of device permission status.

8. Children's Privacy
