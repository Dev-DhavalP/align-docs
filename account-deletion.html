1. How to Request Account Deletion

You can initiate account deletion directly within the Align mobile application ("App"):

  • Open the Align app
  • Go to Account
  • Select Deactivate Account
  • Confirm your request
ℹ️ Account deletion begins with an immediate account deactivation and a 180-day recovery period.

2. What Happens After Deactivation?

Upon requesting account deactivation, the following changes occur:

  • Your account is immediately deactivated and you are signed out of the App.
  • Your account enters a 180-day recovery period.
  • You may reactivate your account by signing in again within the 180-day window.
  • If reactivated during this period, eligible data will be fully restored.

3. Data Deleted After 180 Days

If you do not reactivate your account within 180 days of deactivation, the following data will be permanently and irreversibly deleted from our database servers:

  • Reminder data (bill names, amounts, due dates, payment frequencies, categories, notes)
  • Notification logs and history
  • Push notification tokens

4. Data That May Be Retained

Certain information may be retained for up to 8 years, or longer if required by applicable tax, accounting, fraud prevention, legal, regulatory, or security obligations:

  • Name and email address
  • Subscription history and transaction references
  • Payment and billing records processed through Razorpay

Retained records are stored securely and used only for the purposes described in our Privacy Policy and Terms & Conditions.
